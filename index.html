MEU NOME É AUGUSTO E EU AMO DESENVOLVIMENTO DE SOFTWARE .

I am a freelance graphic designer based in Denver, Colorado. I am passionate about design and illustration and I want to help you make your next project be beautiful.

I graduated from Iowa State University in Ames, Iowa with a B.F.A in graphic design. Since then I have worked as a freelancer multiple times, worked as a designer for HVAC interface systems, worked retail at Jo-ann fabrics, made 8 costumes (for myself, thank you very much, and the number continues growing), and been a professional superhero.

When I’m not doing art you can find me sewing costumes, playing video games, watching anime, writing, reading, or hanging out with my cat.
